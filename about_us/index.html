Our clan

The Wolfheart Tribe, known for their fierce warriors and deep connection to nature, emerged from the rugged fjords of Norway during the late 8th century. Renowned for their exceptional hunting skills and loyalty to their kin, they forged alliances with neighboring clans, becoming formidable players in the region's power struggles. Their legacy, steeped in tales of bravery and spiritual wisdom, continues to inspire descendants and honor the ancient Viking traditions.

As the Wolfheart Tribe expanded their territories, they developed a rich culture centered around storytelling, music, and intricate wood carvings that depicted their legendary ancestors and the gods they revered. The tribe's chieftains often consulted shamans, who guided them through rituals to ensure protection and favor from the spirits of the land. In the face of external threats, the Wolfheart warriors stood united, embodying their motto, "Strength through Unity," which forged an unbreakable bond among their people.
